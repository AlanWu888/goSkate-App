S.K.A.T.E Dice

Don’t know what tricks to use in a game of SKATE? Or do you want to test your flat ground skating abilities? The S.K.A.T.E dice in the goSkate app can be used to solve these problems! The S.K.A.T.E dice allows you to randomly roll tricks from your own list or from a pre-set list of tricks with different levels of difficulty built into them.

To get started with the S.K.A.T.E dice, choose to play with your own list of tricks or from a pre-set list.


Playing with a pre-set list

If you have chosen to play with a pre-set list, simply pick from one of the options listed to you. This will then take you to the SKATE screen.


Playing with a custom list

If you have chosen to make your own custom list of tricks, you will be taken to a screen which allows you to add tricks to a list. To get started with this, simply press the input box at the bottom of the screen to bring up your keyboard, type in a trick and press the “add trick” button. Once you have entered at least 5 tricks, you will be able to start playing your custom game of S.K.A.T.E using the “play” button located at the top of the screen.


Playing S.K.A.T.E

To start your game of S.K.A.T.E, press the “Start” button to roll the first trick of the game. Once this is pressed, a trick will be displayed to you to attempt in the top half of the screen; give the trick an attempt. If you land the trick with the first attempt, press the green tick button; otherwise press the red cross button. Pressing either of these buttons will advance the game by rolling the next trick for you to try, however pressing the red cross will give you a letter.

The game ends when you collect 5 letters (S, K, A, T, E) or if you successfully complete all the tricks in the list! Remember though that before getting your last letter (E), you have two tries of the final trick.


Good luck!
