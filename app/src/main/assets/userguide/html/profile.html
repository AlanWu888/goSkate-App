User Profiles

When pressing onto the profile tab on launching the app for the first time, you will be taken to the login screen where existing users can log back into their accounts with their email and password.


Register a new account

However, new users will have the option to Register an account to the app.

When choosing this option, you will be taken to a new screen which asks you to fill in some fields to create your account. You will be asked to provide the following:

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Password (and confirmation of this password)

After these details have been entered, you will be taken to your profile.


Your Profile

On your profile, you have the ability to see your location contribution counter and review counter! Additionally you have the ability to Edit your profile, where you can change your username, or change your biography.


Edit profile

On this page, you can change your username and/or biography. Firstly, type in your new username or biography, then press the “Submit Changes” button to lock in the changes.

If you wish to only edit one field, you may leave the other empty!


Why should I make an account?

Making an account allows you to submit new locations off for approval by the moderation team. If you want to add your own location to the map, you will need an account to do so!
